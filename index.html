Berserk is a dark fantasy manga series, created by Kentaro Miura in 1989. Miura worked on the series until he passed away in 2021, writing a total of 40 volumes. Currently Kouji Mori, one of Miura's lifelong friends, is continuing the series for him. Miura once told Mori the entire plot of Berserk in great detail. Because of this, Mori is currently the only person who knows the true ending of Berserk. Many who have read Berserk consider it to be one of the greatest manga ever written.

Kentaro Miura had written four manga series before Berserk, none of which reaching near the acclamation that Berserk received. He also briefly worked on Hajime no Ippo as an assistant. Between 2006 and 2021, the manga went through multiple hiatuses, until Miura's death. Berserk has sold over 50 million volumes, making it the 45th best selling manga series of all time.

Guts is the main character of Berserk, who is an extremely strong mercenary that travels from group to group in order to survive. He eventually stumbles into Griffith, the leader of a mercenary group, the Band of the Hawk. Curious about Griffith's overwhelming charisma, Guts joins the Band of the Hawk. He fights alongside Griffith and the Band of the Hawk for years, until Griffith decides to betray his own party in an extremely gruesome way to receive immense power. Pushed to the edge of his sanity by rage, Guts now wants nothing but revenge. Will he be able to get it?

The regular manga volumes are paperback. These volumes have been sold by the U.S. publisher, Dark Horse, since 2003. It took 13 years for the volumes written in the early 90's to be available for purchase in the United States. These volumes cost $15 each.

The deluxe omnibus manga volumes are hardcover, and they contain three regular volumes. These deluxe volumes have been sold by the U.S. publisher, Dark Horse, since 2019. Each omnibus volume costs $50. This means each "volume" in the omnibus costs almost $17. Of course, this higher price per volume means higher quality. The spine is stitched, and the overall page size is increased, as well as higher quality paper.
